# Spaghetti Bolognese

## Ingredients
- 200g spaghetti
- 100g pancetta
- 2 large eggs
- 50g pecorino cheese
- 50g parmesan
- Freshly ground black pepper
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Cook the spaghetti.
2. Fry the pancetta.
3. Beat the eggs and mix with cheese.
4. Combine spaghetti with pancetta and egg mixture.
5. Season with pepper and serve.
